We live in a world where there is an abundance of information that may overwhelm and make it difficult for patterns to be seen. At Sectors, our aim is to provide a service that allows relationships between news events and data to be easily recognised by compressing key data from various sources into one place.

Search via sector and see information on the companies belonging to that sector in one place. Or search via company and/or topics in a time frame to see specific news articles. Each article has related data alongside it as well as links to the companies involved.
